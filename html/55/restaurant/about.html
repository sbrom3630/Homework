Home to the greatest burgers on earth!

Established in 1954, Burger House has been a beloved cornerstone of our community for over six decades. Founded by George and Mary Thompson, the restaurant started as a small, family-run diner with a simple mission: to serve the best burgers in town with a smile. George's secret family recipes and Mary's warm hospitality quickly made Burger House a local favorite.

Through the years, Burger House has stayed true to its roots while evolving to meet the tastes of new generations. Despite changes and expansions, the heart of Burger House remains the same—offering delicious, high-quality burgers made from locally-sourced ingredients. Today, the third generation of the Thompson family continues to uphold the traditions and values that have made Burger House a timeless classic.

From the vintage photos on the walls to the nostalgic flavors in every bite, Burger House is more than just a restaurant—it's a cherished piece of history. Join us and be part of our enduring story, one delicious burger at a time.

Did you know?

George and Mary Thompson, the founders of Burger House, were both avid travelers who drew inspiration from their culinary adventures around the world. Before opening the restaurant, they embarked on a year-long road trip across America in a vintage Airstream trailer. During their journey, they visited countless diners and burger joints, collecting recipes and ideas that would later shape the iconic menu of Burger House. Their favorite discovery? A secret blend of spices from a small roadside diner in Texas, which became the signature seasoning for Burger House's famous patties!
